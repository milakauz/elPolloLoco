El Pollo Loco

In El Pollo Loco you step into the shoes of Pepe Peligroso.

Pepe is a fearless farmer without wings. After being enchanted by his brother-in-law and giving up meat, his chickens have become confused, angry, and unpredictable. They multiply rapidly and want to kill Pepe unless he slaughters them.

Pepe Peligroso, once a lover of grilled chicken, now armed with bottles full of magical potion, enters the battle against the chickens for his farm and for the freedom of vegetarians.
